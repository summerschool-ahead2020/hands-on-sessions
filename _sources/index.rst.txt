AHEAD2020 High-resolution spectroscopy school
=============================================

This is the `AHEAD2020 High-resolution spectroscopy school 
<https://webserver.javalab.ua.es/ahead2020/schools/ahead2020-high-resolution-x-ray-spectroscopy-school>`_ 
website for the hands-on sessions. You can find the instructions and exercises here for various X-ray astronomy topics.
Please find the course materials on `our Github page <https://github.com/summerschool-ahead2020>`_
and `our Zenodo community <https://zenodo.org/communities/ahead2020_alicante>`_.
